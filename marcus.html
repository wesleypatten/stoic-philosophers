Marcus Aurelius

Marcus Aurelius was a Roman emperor and a Stoic philosopher, best known for his work Meditations, which offers insights into personal ethics and self-discipline. His reign from 161 to 180 AD was marked by wisdom, resilience, and dedication to duty.
